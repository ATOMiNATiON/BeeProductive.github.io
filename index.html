Privacy Policy

We do not collect or store any personal information. Anything that could be used to identify a user isn’t even seen by us, or anyone else but the user. We do not work with or use third party hosting, thus no information is stored through third party platforms. No names, emails, locations, device information, financial details, birthdays or any other personal information at all are stored or sent. We do not use any form of third party database to store user data, nor do we use bug reports that collect any data from your device. We do not use accounts, thus your email, name, or password that would be used for accounts aren’t saved.
